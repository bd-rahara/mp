ABOUT

MedPlus, founded in 2006 by Dr. Madhukar Gangadi, is one of India's largest pharmacy chains. Starting with a single store in Hyderabad, it has expanded to over 3,000 locations across the country. The company's growth is driven by its commitment to quality, affordability, and accessibility.
MedPlus offers a range of services, including pharmacy products, diagnostic tests through MedPlus Labs, and an online pharmacy platform for convenient home delivery. Its extensive network ensures that medications and health products are readily available, bridging gaps in healthcare accessibility, especially in urban and semi-urban areas.
